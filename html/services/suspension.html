Suspension System Inspection & Repair

A properly functioning suspension system is crucial for your vehicle's safety, handling, and comfort. Our comprehensive suspension diagnostics and repair services cover everything from computerized wheel alignment to component replacement, using state-of-the-art Hunter Elite equipment and premium OEM or high-quality aftermarket parts. We identify worn components like ball joints, shock absorbers, and bushings before they cause bigger problems, restoring your vehicle's factory handling characteristics.

Regular suspension maintenance every 30,000 km can prevent costly repairs down the road and significantly extend the life of your tires and other components. Our technicians not only fix existing issues but also provide valuable advice on preserving your suspension, such as avoiding potholes, washing salt off in winter, and replacing parts in pairs to maintain balance.
