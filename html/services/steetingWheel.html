Steering System Diagnostics & Repair

Your vehicle's steering system requires regular maintenance to ensure responsive control and driving safety. Our expert technicians perform thorough diagnostics to detect issues like fluid leaks, worn tie rods, or electronic steering faults, using specialized tools to measure play and pressure in all components.

Whether you need a simple power steering fluid flush or a complete steering rack replacement, we use OEM-quality parts and follow manufacturer specifications for every repair. Our services include everything from fixing leaks and replacing worn bushings to recalibrating sensitive electronic steering components after collisions. With clear diagnostics and upfront pricing, we’ll restore your steering’s smooth operation so you can drive with confidence. Don’t ignore warning signs like stiffness, unusual noises, or warning lights – timely repairs ensure your safety on the road.
